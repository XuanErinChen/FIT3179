Wildlife Trade Visualisations

The proportional symbol maps show spatical distribution of wildlife importer over the world from 2016 to 2017. The size of circles represent number of trade for import. When moving mouse pointer onto the circles, the tooltips will show the name of importer, number of trade imported and total quantity traded from import.

The stacked bar chart shows the top 4 wildlife species trade numbers over the world from 2016 to 2017 and proportion of goods catergory in different Species.Clicking on one of species on the top right box will highlight that specific species. The filters under it can be used and "Appendix" means animal protection class. Appendix I species are the most protected. More definitions can be found from Guide created by CITES.

This visualisation is created by Xuan Chen. The data source is from Kaggle.
